India’s Kochi is home to Muthoot Finance Ltd, which was established in 1939. The Company offers loans (finance) secured by gold jewellery as security. Financing and Power Generation make up the Company’s two business sectors. To help those who lack access to formal credit fulfil their short-term working cash needs, the company offers personal and business loans (backed by gold jewellery) to them most often. The company provides domestic money transfer services, quick money transfer services, white-label automated teller machines, insurance broking, house loans, personal loans, collection services, microfinance, windmill power production, international inbound money transfer services, and gold loans. Over 4,294 of the company’s branches are spread throughout India.
